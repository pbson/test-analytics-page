About MegaStack

We're on a mission to make cloud computing effortless. Every feature, every integration, every API — built for maximum developer productivity.

Our Mission

Founded in 2023, MegaStack was born from a simple idea: developers should never have to think about infrastructure. We believe that every deployment should be instant, every feature should work out of the box, and every developer should ship faster.

Our platform gives developers all the tools they need in one place — hosting, databases, analytics, CI/CD, monitoring, and more. No more stitching together 15 different services.

We operate data centers on 3 continents with redundant infrastructure, ensuring maximum uptime and the lowest latency for every workload, no matter how big.
